How It Works

Two Components

1. Monthly Drill

One weekend a month at your local unit. This is required. It's where you learn fundamentals, get fitted for uniforms, and build your team.

2. Advanced Training

Summer & Winter breaks. Aviation, SCUBA, Robotics, and more. Unlocked ONLY after qualifying at your unit.
